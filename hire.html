Your event, your moment

This popular dance style originating from Turkey and Egypt has made its way across the Western world, achieving a level of popularity unheard of for many other solo dance forms. Our Belly Dancers can perform a mesmerising act to music for your guests to watch, or perhaps you will want them to get up and learn some moves! Hire professional Belly dancers to bring a flavour of the Middle East to your event or spice up a party with Belly Dance classes for Hen parties and birthday parties. Tell us a bit more about your event, and we will happily advise you on the right options, from Belly dancers for weddings to Belly dancers for Tourist Board events and exhibitions.
